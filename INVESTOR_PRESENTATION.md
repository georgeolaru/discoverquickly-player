# Discover Quickly Queue Player

Discover Quickly Queue Player is a lightweight Chrome extension built for serious music discovery users. It turns the fragmented preview experience inside Discover Quickly into a continuous, high-speed listening workflow: users can queue an entire playlist or artist section, listen hands-free, bookmark standout tracks in real time, and jump back to their finds instantly.

The value is simple but powerful. Instead of hovering track by track and losing momentum, users move through dozens of previews as a structured stream. Playback controls, keyboard shortcuts, inline launch buttons, and saved bookmarks reduce friction and make discovery sessions faster, more focused, and more repeatable.

From an investor perspective, this is an early but very clear wedge product: a workflow tool for highly engaged music explorers, curators, DJs, label scouts, and tastemakers. It solves a narrow problem extremely well, sits directly inside an existing behavior-rich platform, and opens the door to broader discovery productivity features such as smarter save lists, collaboration, ranking, export, and cross-platform music research workflows.

In short, this extension is not trying to replace streaming platforms. It is building the productivity layer on top of music discovery, starting with a painful niche workflow and turning it into a faster, stickier, higher-value user experience.
